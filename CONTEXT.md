# Aviously

Aviously is Avi's personal portfolio website: who he is, what he's built, and how to reach him. A soft "I take on work" angle is present but secondary to the portfolio. The site speaks in one snarky voice (the Aviously Voice) and keeps its language organized around human collaboration and practical software craft.

## Language

**Aviously Voice**:
The site's single snarky voice and signature pun. Its tic is the word "obviously," spelled **aviously** (a play on Avi's name and the `aviously.me` domain). The default is a literal swap — every "obviously" becomes "aviously" — but copy stores the canonical word and a render-time toggle ("the hook") can revert it back to "obviously" where the real word is wanted. Snark, not cruelty: confident and dry, never mean.
_Avoid_: Sarcastic, edgy, brand voice, tone of voice, gag

**The Hook**:
The toggle that controls the obviously↔aviously swap. Copy is authored with the canonical word and rendered through the swap unless the hook turns it off. Some instances are **pinned** — proper-name puns like the hero title "Hi, aviously I'm Avi." always render "aviously" regardless of the toggle, because there the word is the joke, not the adverb. Also the seam left in place so multiple voices/personas could return later without rewriting content.
_Avoid_: Feature flag, config, i18n

**Fully-Formed AI-Assisted Human**:
Avi's signature self-label and the snarky long form of [[AI-Assisted Human]]. "Fully-formed" is the joke aimed at the AI-bro genre — a complete person who happens to use the robots, not a personality generated by them.
_Avoid_: AI persona, digital human, assistant

**Embedded Automation Partner**:
Avi's flagship professional offer: he joins a company for a defined period, learns how people actually work, and builds practical software or AI-assisted systems that reduce friction.
_Avoid_: Consultant, freelancer, dev-for-hire, AI expert

**Old-School Service, Modern Tools**:
The public-facing professional offer: attentive, practical service paired with building practical software and AI-assisted systems, shaped around how a business already works.
_Avoid_: AI transformation, digital transformation, workflow optimization

**AI-Assisted Human**:
The site's lead identity and professional positioning, not just a stance: Avi is a human who stays in charge while using AI with curiosity, humor, and judgment. The contrarian alternative to "AI engineer / AI-powered / AI-first." Anchored by concrete proof of senior-dev-who-builds work so it reads as a person with receipts, not a manifesto.
_Avoid_: AI-powered, AI-first, anti-AI, AI engineer, prompt engineer

**Experiential Input**:
A site interaction where asking the visitor for information becomes a small creative tool or memorable exchange, rather than a plain form field.
_Avoid_: Contact form, lead capture, funnel

**Think and Feel**:
The purpose of an experiential input: encourage visitors to clarify what is happening in their business while making the exchange feel human, useful, and a little memorable.
_Avoid_: Qualify leads, extract requirements, conversion

**Business Type**:
A visitor's plain-language description of what kind of business they run, gathered through an interesting choice interaction instead of a sterile industry dropdown.
_Avoid_: Industry vertical, segment, persona

**Duct-Tape Work**:
The patched-together business work that technically functions but depends on fragile handoffs, manual fixes, spreadsheets, copied messages, exports, or one person knowing the trick.
_Avoid_: Workflow, pain point, automation opportunity, process inefficiency

**Human Persona**:
A selectable voice and point of view for the site, used to explore different ways Avi can speak to visitors while staying grounded in the same underlying offer.
_Avoid_: Theme, language, copy variant, user persona

**The Guy**:
The default Human Persona: plainspoken, useful, lightly snarky, and grounded in the promise that some duct-tape business problems need a capable person who can listen and build the thing.
_Avoid_: Consultant, guru, thought leader

**Supaplex-Inspired World**:
The site's visual and interaction atmosphere: a retro PC-game world of tiles, circuits, sound, and playful controls that supports the business message without turning the homepage into a full game.
_Avoid_: Theme, gimmick, clone

**Owner-Operated Business**:
A small or mid-sized business where the person making the decision is still close to the daily work, customer relationships, staff, and operational bottlenecks.
_Avoid_: Founder-led startup, SMB, target account
